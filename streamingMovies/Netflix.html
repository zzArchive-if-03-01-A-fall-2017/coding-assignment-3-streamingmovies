Netflix

Information

Foundation: 1997

Owner: Netflix Inc.

Location: Los Gatos, USA

CEO: Reed Hastings

Slogan: It just might bring everyone together.

Cost: 11€/month


History

1997Reed Hastings and software exceutive Marc Randolph co-founded Netflix to offer online movie rentals.
1998Netflix launched the first DVD rental and sale site, netflix.com.
1999Netflix debured a subscription service, offering unlimted DVD rentals for one low monthly price.
2000Netflix inroduced a personalised movie recommendation system, which uses Netflix members' ratings to accurately predict for the users.
2002Netflix made its intial public offering with 600,000 members in the US.
2005The number of users has risen to 4.2 million.
2007Netflix introduced streaming, which allows members to instantly watch television shows and movies on their personal computers.
2008Netflix partnered with consumer electronics companies to stream on the Xbox 360, Blu-ray disc players and TV set-top boxes.
2009Netflix partnered with consumer electronics companies to stream on the PS3, Internet connected TVs and other Internet connected devices.
2010Netflix got available on the Apple iPad, iPhone and iPod Touch, the Nintendo Wii, and other Internet connected devices. Netflix launched its service in Canada.
2011Netflix launched throughout Latin America and the Caribbean.
2012Netflix became available in Europe including the United Kingdom, Ireland and the Nordic Countries. Netflix won its first Primetime Emmy Engineering Award.
2013Netflix expanded to the Netherlands. Netflix garners 31 primetime Emmy nominations including outstanding drama series, comedy series and documentary or nonfiction special for “House of Cards”, “Orange Is The New Black”, and “The Square” respectively. House of Cards won three Primetime Emmy Awards. Netflix was the first internet TV network nominated for the primetime Emmy
2014Netflix launched in 6 new countries in Europe (Austria, Belgium, France, Germany, Luxembourg and Switzerland). Netflix won 7 creative Emmy Awards for "House of Cards" and "Orange Is The New Black". Netflix now had over 50 million members globally.
2015Netflix launched in Australia, New Zealand and Japan, with continued expansion across Europe in Italy, Spain and Portugal. The first Netflix original feature film "Beasts of No Nation" is released
2016 Netflix got available worldwide.
